Hi! I'm Bruce Marshall.

I'm an 18 year old who is interested in web development. I live on a hobby farm, where we raise some sheep and Highland Cows. After completing my Associate's Degree, I plan to transfer to Missouri State University and complete a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science. In my free time I enjoy coding, playing chess, reading, tanning hides, or a multitude of other things.
